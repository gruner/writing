I don't normally do this, but for this illustration I wanted to paint it so that the emerging details would reinforce the message of the final image, telling a story of sorts. I started with the background and the person in the middle praying. Then the tubes and pipes slowly encroach on him until the end when they're all screaming in unison for his attention.

## Some Notes

The phone was originally going to be an old-timey stock ticker. I changed it at the advice of my wife, who convinced me that no one would know what it was supposed to be. (Although it won't be much longer before rotary phones are in the same boat.)
